An academic with over 10 years of international experience in interdisciplinary computer engineering, artificial intelligence (AI) and wireless networks. Currently Lecturer and Programme Leader within the Computer Science Department at Middlesex University London. Strong teaching portfolio, innovative design and delivery of modules and assessments/feedback, strong interest in Gen-AI in HE STEM, and strong publication record in high-impact journals, experienced in EU-funded projects, and passionate about inclusive, student-centered teaching. He is a Fellow of the Advance Higher Education Academy (FHEA).
